Dear participant,

In this lie detection study, participants see various videos and texts, and they have to decide whether the given material is truthful or deception. The test takes around 10-20 minutes (depending on the randomly assigned test material). A valid participation via MTurk will be rewarded with $2.00 gross. Repeated participation is not allowed.

You may freely decide to discontinue the experiment at any time without consequences. In that case, none of you test data will be retained.

Your personal and demographic data will be treated in full confidentiality. The experiment data (truth/lie judgments, etc.) may be shared only completely anonymously in the scientific community.

This experimental program has been thoroughly tested and proven stable many times, but we do not take responsibility for potential unexpected technical issues.

The study is organized at the Department of Psychology of the University of Gothenburg, in cooperation with the Department of Basic Psychological Research and Research Methods at the University of Vienna.
